Meet Kimberly Flores

Hello! I recently graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Informatics and specialization in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). What's Informatics you ask? It's designing software based on how people interact with technologies. During my time, my passion for creating user-centric designs started and I dove into the world of User Experience Design and Research. I gained techniques on how to create such designs by getting to know my users first and getting them involved in the process. I then honed my skills in web development. I've built out apps with a team and on my own. With both design and code, I am able to create a product that my users like. I am currently looking for a career in front-end development or UX/UI design.
