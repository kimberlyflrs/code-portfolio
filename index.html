About Me

Hello! My name is Kimberly Flores I recently graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Informatics and specialization in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

Creating user-centric interfaces became one of my favorite things to create during my time in school. I learned about user experience design, research techniques, and how these interfaces are created through code. With both design and code, I am able to create an inclusive product that my users and clients will enjoy.

I am a determined person who is eager to learn new skills and help out others. In my free time, I enjoy taking a walk, exploring hiking trails, trying to cook new things, and painting new things.

I am currently looking for a career in web development or UX/UI design.
